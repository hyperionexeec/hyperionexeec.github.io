No, you will have to buy another key once your time ends.

Will we be compensated downtime?

Yes, you will be compensated for all downtime, along with any bugs that make Hyperion unusable for the majority.

Can I get a refund?

You will only be refunded if you have an issue we are unable to fix or have had it for less than three days.
